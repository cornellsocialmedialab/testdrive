Use Social Media TestDrive in your research

Social Media TestDrive is built on proven effective learning theories and can serve as a foundation for numerous research questions.

If you decide to use TestDrive in your research, we request that you complete the following questionnaire. This questionnaire is a non-binding survey that informs us about how you plan to use TestDrive and gives us a better understanding of how researchers are using the platform.

Social Media TestDrive for Researchers - Questionnaire

Additionally, when you have completed your study, please remember to cite TestDrive (see the first publication below) and to share an end-of-study report with us at socialmediatestdrive@gmail.com. We would love to hear your findings!

Publications and resources

Feel free to use our published research and findings to guide your research study. The following papers give an overview and history of Social Media TestDrive, as well as the underlying theory behind the platform. The last paper also provides a developed and validated inventory of social media literacy measures.

DiFranzo, D., Choi, Y.H., Purington, A., Taft, J.G., Whitlock, J., Bazarova, N.N. (2019). Social Media TestDrive: Real-World social media education for the next generation. In Proceedings of the 2019 ACM Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems (CHI’19). Glasgow, UK.

Purington Drake, A., Masur, P.K., Bazarova, N.N., Zou, E., & Whitlock, J. (in press). The Youth Social Media Literacy Scale: Scale Development and Validation Using Item Response Theory. Journal of Children and Media.
