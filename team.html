Team

Social Media TestDrive was created by a multidisciplinary team of researchers, designers, and developers at Cornell's Social Media Lab, and a collaboration with Common Sense Education.

Core Team

Dr. Natalie Bazarova. Dr. Bazarova is the project co-founder and the current Project Director. She is an Associate Professor in the Department of Communication and the Director of the Social Media Lab at Cornell University. She can be contacted at nnb8@cornell.edu.

Dr. Janis Whitlock. Dr. Whitlock is the project co-founder and currently contributes to module development and evaluation oversight. She is an Associate Director and a Research Scientist at Cornell's Bronfenbrenner Center for Translational Research.

Dr. Yoon Hyung Choi. Dr. Choi is the on-site manager for all program activities and leads coordination of content development. She is a Postdoctoral Associate in the Department of Communication at Cornell University.

Dr. Dominic DiFranzo. Dr. DiFranzo is the developer of the TestDrive platform and advises the technical development team. He is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Lehigh University.

Amanda Purington. Ms. Purington contributes to content development and is conducting an outcome evaluation on TestDrive as part of her doctorate in Communication. She is the Director of Evaluation and Research at ACT for Youth project in the Bronfenbrenner Center for Translational Research and a graduate student in the Department of Communication at Cornell University.

Anna Spring. Ms. Spring is the TestDrive application developer. She is an Applications Programmer in the Department of Communication at Cornell University.

Dr. Dan Cosley. Dr. Cosley provides support for the design and content development of the TestDrive project. Dr. Cosley is an Associate Professor of Information Science at Cornell University.

Ashley Shea. Ms. Shea provides support for the design and content development of the TestDrive project. She is the Head of Instruction Initiatives at Albert R. Mann Library at Cornell University.
