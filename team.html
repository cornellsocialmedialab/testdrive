Team

Social Media TestDrive was created by a multidisciplinary team of researchers, designers, and developers at Cornell's Social Media Lab, and a collaboration with Common Sense Education.

Core Team

Dr. Natalie Bazarova is the Social Media TestDrive Project Co-Founder and Director. She is a Professor in the Department of Communication and the Director of the Social Media Lab at Cornell University. She can be contacted at nnb8@cornell.edu.

Dr. Janis Whitlock is the Social Media TestDrive Project Co-Founder and currently contributes to module evaluation oversight. She is an Emerita Research Scientist at Cornell University.

Dr. Dominic DiFranzo is the developer of the TestDrive platform and advises the technical development team. He is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Lehigh University.

Amanda Purington contributes to content development and is conducting an outcome evaluation on TestDrive as part of her doctorate in Communication. She is the Director of Evaluation and Research at ACT for Youth project in the Bronfenbrenner Center for Translational Research and a graduate student in the Department of Communication at Cornell University.

Dr. Ellen (Wenting) Zou is involved in the evaluation, redesign, and analytics of TestDrive. She is currently leading the development of an AI-based conversational agent system to provide personalized feedback to learners on TestDrive. She is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Educational Psychology, Counseling, and Special Education at Pennsylvania State University.

Winice Hui is the TestDrive application developer. She is currently an Applications Programmer in the Department of Communication at Cornell University.

Eunice Han assists with the outcome evaluation study on TestDrive and contributes to TestDrive's outreach and professional development efforts. She is currently the lab manager for the Social Media Lab in the Department of Communication at Cornell University.
