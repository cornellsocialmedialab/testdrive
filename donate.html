Social Media TestDrive is created by a multidisciplinary team of researchers, designers, and developers at Cornell University's Social Media Lab in collaboration with Common Sense Education.

It is a free resource, so we invite your support to fund its continual operation and addition of new features.

Join us in supporting our mission to prepare young people for the online world, and we thank you for your generous support!
